LANSuiteA Content Management System designed especially for the needs of LAN-Parties

Get Started
GitHub

Organisation of LAN-Parties

Announce a new LAN-Party and enable people to sign up with a direct payment flow and several follow up actions like Clan creation and more.

Organisation of tournaments

Manage tournaments for multiple games with different modes like single- and double-elimination, league or group games with KO strategy.

Seat plans

Define a seating plan with several rooms and areas and let LAN Party attendees choose their seat in advance. This enables clans to sit together to facilitate better team play.

Projector support

Show the latest content like news messages, the current state of a tournament or a timetable at a wall via the projector mode during the party to inform attendees.
